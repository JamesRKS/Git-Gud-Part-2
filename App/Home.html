Summary

'

The world of Imagin is powered by THE ARTIST, an artistic human from earth, randomly chosen when the previous artist dies. However, the Artist of previous years have been either evil or killed by the deadly creations of previous artists. Enter the Protectors Guild, a group of three powerful characters to protect the 326th Artist from dangers. These brave three are CALVIN GREY, SKYLAR YOUNG, and SOPHOCLES THE GHOST. Watch their adventures, both the fantastical and the mundane, as they fight powerful enemies. But nothing THAT BAD could happen, right?
